District Visits & Events

The district calendar includes:

  • Official Visitations: Scheduled visits of the District Deputy Grand Master (DDGM) to the lodges.
  • District Meetings: Council of Masters and district-wide conventions.
  • Degree Work: Initiations, Passings, and Raisings across the 11 lodges.
  • Charity Events: Public conventions, fundraisers, and charitable drives.

Lodge officers are requested to submit schedule updates to the District Grand Director of Ceremonies.
